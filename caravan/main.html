ABOUT

People tend to come across many animals or people that they would like to help but cannot immediately,due to lack of time. If one finds an injured , malnourished animal or a homeless person in need of helpwhile on their way to work, or to school, they cannot stop to help out. CARAVAN is a social app that lets people share a request for help online with people who can help. It aims to make the community help each other out, rather than relying on the local municipal corporations or NGOs each time.
